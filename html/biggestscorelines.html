Arsenal's biggest victory margin in the Premier League era is 7-0


Since the Premier League was formed in August 1992 as the leading football league competition in England, Arsenal's biggest winning scoreline is 7-0, achieved on two occations. These two matches both occurred in the latter days of Arsenal playing at Highbury, firstly against Everton in May 2005 and secondly against Middlesbrough in January 2006.

Arsenal have scored 7 goals on two other occasions in the Premier League, with a 7-1 victory over Blackburn Rovers in February 2012 and a 7-3 victory over Newcastle United in December 2012.

In away matches in the Premier League, Arsenal have never scored more than 6 goals in a match, achieving this feat on three occasions. Arsenal beat Middlesbrough 6-1 in April 1999, Derby County 6-2 in April 2008 and Everton 6-1 in August 2009.

One unenviable statistic about Arsenal in the Premier League era is that they have never scored more than 7 goals in a match, but have conceded 8 in a match, falling to an 8-2 defeat to Manchester United at Old Tafford in August 2011.
