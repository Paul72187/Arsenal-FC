Eight Arsenal players have scored on their Premier League debut

Since the Premier League was formed in August 1992 as the leading football league competition in England, eight Arsenal players have scored on their league debut.

The first player to score on his Arsenal debut in the Premier League was Fredrik Ljungberg. He scored the final goal in a comfortable 3-0 win over Manchester United at Highbury in September 1998. Ironically, Ljungberg did not register another Premier League goal until almost a year later in the corresponding fixture. However, despite giving Arsenal the lead in August 1999, a Roy Keane brace ensured Manchester United secured a 1-2 away win.

Other notable debut scorers include regular goalscorers such as Emmanuel Adebayor, Alexandre Lacazette and Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang. However, some unusual and rare goalscorers registered a debut goal for the Gunners including Gabriel Magalhaes, Bisan Lauren and Cedric Soares. For Cedric Soares, his debut goal would be only one of two Premier League goals scored in 38 appearances.

Arsenal have won all eight matches in the Premier League in which there has been a debutant goalscorer.
